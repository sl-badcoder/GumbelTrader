What You Practice

Sequence drills help you identify arithmetic changes, geometric changes, alternating rules, and mixed patterns. The goal is to recognize the rule quickly, then enter the next value accurately.

Example Patterns

  • Arithmetic progressions such as 7, 11, 15, 19, ...
  • Geometric progressions such as 3, 6, 12, 24, ...
  • Alternating and layered rules that require a second look.
